Welcome to Tax Defence

 

Tax Defence is a locally owned and operated business in Australia, run by fully qualified accountant Deb Porteous. Deb has over 25 years’ experience in the finance industry and offers a full range of tax and accounting services.

During the months of July to October Deb specialises in defence force and veteran tax returns, although she welcomes anyone from individuals, to SME's to have their tax returns done.

At Tax Defence we recognise the burden that tax puts on you, and work with you to minimise your tax position. We take the time to understand your job to ensure we maximise your deductions and offsets. Our method of showing and explaining how to get the most out of your tax refund means that our clients’ come back year after year.
